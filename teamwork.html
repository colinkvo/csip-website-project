Teamwork is Key, In and Out of Class

When we work together, we often bounce ideas, openining up new approaches to different problems. As classmates, our goal is to not only succeed ourselves, but also to help our peers succeed as well. We help each other study for exams, bounce ideas for programs, and many other things. Using the app Discord, we communicate with each other through a class server, whether it's sharing ideas, sharing pictures of the homework, or links to help study or learn.
