Why Cybersecurity?

Every single day, billions of technological devices are used around the world. The world revolves around technology, and as we become more reliant on it, our information goes cyber. Cybersecurity plays a vital role in keeping our information secure and protected.



Examples of Cybersecurity in Our Everyday Lives:

  • Password Encryption
    • In order to keep our information safe, we need strong passwords, which is, although very simple, a vital part to cybersecurity.

  • Network Security
    • To prevent malicious attacks on our networks we use everyday, methods of cybersecurity are used to protect them.

  • Application Security
    • Similar to network security, cybersecurity methods are applied in applications to ensure that threats cannot take the information attackers are looking for.
