About Us

As CSIP students, we study a variety of tech-related content, ranging from IT to Programming, all closely related to Cybersecurity. We attend classes at the County College of Morris in the mornings, and return to our highschools for our regularly scheduled classes. Coming from all different schools and districts, all 19 of us come together as one class - one big Cyber family.



Things We Do:

  • Learn about and practice hands-on with computers in lab activities
  • Learn and write programs in Java, Python, Visual Basic, and more
  • Learn about the Windows, MacOS, Linux, and Unix operating systems
  • Go on field trips to learn about Cybersecurity and related fields
